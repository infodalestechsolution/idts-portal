Job description

Position : AEM Developer

Experience : (3-6 yrs)

Location : Nagpur

Responsibilities :

  • Work with development teams to implement integrated technical solutions.
  • Develop code in a timely and high quality manner while leveraging standards including site
  • structure, page templates, navigation and the site information architecture.
  • Build AEM authoring components, templates, workflows.
  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable and stable web solutions that meet business needs.
  • Utilize AEM to layout pages, troubleshoot issues, and train authors.
  • Partner with Visual Design and User Experience teams to develop effective/ engaging designs.
  • Utilize reusable templates and common HTML elements while leveraging centralized JQuery/ JavaScript libraries and global CSS.
  • Provide high quality documentation for your work.

Qualifications :

3+ years of IT experience with 2+ years of experience within AEM implementing Web Content Management (WCM) and Digital Asset Management (DAM) solutions.

  • AEM Developer certification is a must.
  • Experience in AEM component development, workflow development and integrating AEM with other applications.
  • Expertise in AEM and associated technologies such as Sling, OSGI, JCR. Experience with Java technologies and frameworks.
  • Experience to other web technologies like Search, XML, XSL etc. Excellent communication skills
