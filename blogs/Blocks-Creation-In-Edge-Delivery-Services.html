What is Block?

Block is a modular, reusable unit of content or functionality that is designed to streamline content delivery and personalization at the edge. Blocks are the building blocks for rendering web pages or delivering experiences directly at the edge, enabling faster delivery and reduced latency by leveraging edge computing principles
